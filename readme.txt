Help stop Anti-Counterfeiting Trade Agreement by putting a ribbon that says Stop ACTA on your WordPress site or network.

== Description ==

A black ribbon with the words "Stop ACTA" will be put on in the top right corner of your website, linking to the [Stop ACTA](http://www.avaaz.org/en/eu_save_the_internet/) website. Look at the screenshots to see how the ribbon looks. Check out the [Help Stop SOPA/PIPA](http://wordpress.org/news/2012/01/help-stop-sopa-pipa/) entry in the WordPress news too. Thank you for showing your support!

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin zip archive.
2. Extract it in your `wp-content/plugins` folder or upload via admin panel.
3. Browse to your plugins section and active the plugin.
